About




Electric picnic is the ultimate festival in Ireland.
Electric Picnic 2004 was the year it all started.
It started as one day event for 10,000 people
on a gloriously sunny September Saturday.
It was billed as a Boutique music festival
and with such a diverse line up of not just
music it promptly sold out.
